Uni Products

Uni Products is an public app that helps the introduces its audience to the variety of products available. It has different features and functions that help in maintaining the progress of work and ease the communication between clients and the company's team.
App Feautres
  • Displays the products available
    • Product Categories
    • Product Brands
    • Product details
  • Creates a record of each client
    • Allow creating new clients
    • Allow editing current clients
  • Generate quotes for current clients
    • Allow creating new quotes for current user clients only
    • Allow to view current quotes
    • Sending quotes through an email serivce

Sign in can be made anonymously or with a user name and password. We will only keep the data you provide, and we will not track you info or location.

The company will be able to supply her clients needs for prices and technical data from within the application when the submit a request to our team, also the clients can access all the products available and get the required information from the app itself

Uni Product app is in a constant progress and will have additional features in the future.
